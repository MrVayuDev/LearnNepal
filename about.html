Our Mission

Empowering Students with Premium Education

LearnNepal is dedicated to providing high-quality, structured, and accessible digital study materials for NEB Class 11 & 12 students across Nepal — completely free.

The LearnNepal Story

Education in the digital age should be beautiful, fast, and accessible. We noticed that most educational resources in Nepal were cluttered, outdated, or difficult to navigate.

We started LearnNepal with a simple goal: to create the "Apple" of educational platforms for Nepali students. A space where learning feels premium, notes are meticulously crafted, and the user experience is fluid.

Quality Content: Verified by subject experts and board patterns.
Zero Ads Clutter: Focus on reading, not closing popups.
Always Free: Accessible to every single student across the nation.
