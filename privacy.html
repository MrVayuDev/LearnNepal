Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to LearnNepal. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting any personal data you share. This privacy policy informs you about how we handle any basic visitor metrics when you access our platform, and details your privacy rights under relevant digital accessibility acts.

2. Information We Collect

Currently, LearnNepal is a completely static, informational website. We do not require you to create an account, register, or provide personal information such as names, phone numbers, or addresses.

However, we may use basic, anonymized web analytics to understand general visitor metrics (e.g., page views, length of visit, device categories) to enhance our educational notes and summaries.

3. Cookies

We may use cookies to customize theme preferences (e.g., remembering your Dark Mode selection) and to cache search indexes for Ctrl+K palette search query speed. You can configure your browser to reject all or some browser cookies. Disabling cookies will only prevent theme memory, and will not break notes reading.

4. Third-Party Links

This platform links to external media and assets (e.g., Google Web Fonts, FontAwesome vectors, and YouTube for embedded video tutorials). Clicking on those links or playing embedded videos may allow third parties to compile standard usage telemetry. We do not control these third-party platforms and strongly advise reviewing their respective privacy outlines.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy statement occasionally to match regulatory changes or new educational feature rolls. We will indicate revisions by updating the "Last Updated" timestamp at the top of this page.

Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, or suggestions for improving data transparency on LearnNepal, please reach out via our dedicated Contact Page.
